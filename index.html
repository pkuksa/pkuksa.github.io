I am a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine, Perelman School of Medicine, University of Pennsylvania. Previously, I was a postdoctoral scientist at NEC Laboratories America, Inc. in the Machine Learning department. Before that, I was working on my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I was affiliated with Rutgers Sequence Analysis and Modeling Lab (SeqAM). My main research interests include applied machine learning, sequence modeling and analysis, biomedical informatics, natural language processing, algorithms, pattern recognition, text and data mining, and computer vision. I got bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering (2002) and M.Sc. in Information and Computer Sciences (2004) from Bauman Moscow State Technical University (Moscow, Russia).
